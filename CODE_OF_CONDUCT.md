# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be friendly, constructive, and respectful.
- Use inclusive language.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Hate speech or exclusion.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers via email: `security@rechain.network` or file an issue (private if needed). All reports will be treated confidentially.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content or ban accounts when necessary.
